
# One Honest Look

One Honest Look helps you visualize who you are today, then intentionally design and work toward who you want to become.

## Product Vision

The product is built to:
- help you map your current patterns, habits, strengths, and struggles
- track progress toward your ideal future self over time
- surface where you are doing well and where you are falling short
- guide you toward systems, practices, and tools that support real change
- preserve a historical archive of your growth so you can reflect on long-term evolution
- make that growth visible through clear, motivating progress views

At its core, the goal is simple: an honest look at yourself that helps you become your best self.

## Platform Roadmap

- **Now:** Browser-optimized web app (desktop and mobile browser friendly)
- **Next:** Native iOS app
- **Then:** Native Android app (Google Play)

## Development

Run `npm run dev` to start the development server.
